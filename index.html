    Where the mountains meet the sea.

    Why Antique?

    The rugged and varied land of Antique offers visitors a variety of outdoor activities. Diving and beach enthusiasts would have a great time discovering the unspoiled islets of Antique.

    I present to you the Wonders of Antique.

    Malumpati Cold Springs

    Malumpati Cold Springs is a very popular place here in Antique. I like to call it heaven, because you're gonna feel like you're in heaven when you get in contact with their refreshing cold water.




    Mararison Island

    Mararison Island, almost everyone in Antique has heard of it, it's literal paradise, from the white sandy beach to the other remaining 55 hectares you sure are gonna enjoy exploring this island!




    Antique Rice Terraces

    Antique Rice Terraces, I haven't been there but I have heard stories, all of them are good. They all say it's beyond beautiful and from the pictures I saw, I can not help but agree.




    Vista Veranda Resort

    Vista Veranda Resort isn't really a wonder but it is truly a beautiful place. From the delectable food to the beatiful beach, you are for sure to be satisfied.




    Aningalan, San Remegio

    Now this, this is truly a beautiful place. From the strawberry farm to the caves you can explore, Aningalan is surely a place you would want to travel to when you go to Antique
